the basics...

Name: Felix 'Flust' Grey
Species: Human
Age: 35
Birthday: December 31
Gender: Male
Pronouns: He/him
Height: 1,75 cm / 5'7" ft
Weight: 120 pounds
Eye Color: Redwood
Hair Color: Crow Black

Voice: husky but flat and with no emotion
Zodiac: Capricorn
Occupation: Private Detective
Fears: never getting his memories back / losing more of them, death and the occult
Hopes: regain his memories, solve the case and have a normal life

After a mysteriously occurring accident during a serial case, Felix lost most of his memories, being thrown in a job he doesn't remember how to do and with the daunting task of reading a gigantic backlog of what he believes to be his own journals, he has become a very silent and reserved man, it's not that he doesn't want to show other emotions, it's mostly that he can't quite remember what he was like to begin with.
With his depressed face and mood being the result of such weird amnesic episode, he tried to not force too many emotions out, with only small, almost sarcastic looking smiles signifying his happiness, and scrunched brows that severely wrinkles his forehead to show anger and disapproval of a topic.

the world...

In this alternate universe, Terror Mountain is a small resort town in the 1930s, contrary to the more popular beach and island destinations of that decade, this beautiful snowy wonderland offered a cool and cozy atmosphere all year long, gaining a lot of traction when summer skiing became a big craze with the youth. But not all is as sweet as the cakes and tea cups served at the Inn, during the summer of 1934 a series of mysterious disappearances began, the first was a teen named Betty, last seen window browsing around the small shopping area; the next was a young adult named Joseph, last seen around the lakeside area of the resort, seemingly trying to fish; and the last disappearance that year was a second teen named Carol, who was reportedly seen sneaking out of her cabin to meet an unknown person before entering an Adler Trumpf modeled convertible car.

After these disappearances and the presence of this mysterious man that sported quite a new model of convertible car, Mayor Ambrewster decided to have a "secret" meeting with the Chef of Police, Charles Richart, both men discussed what could be done behind closed doors as to not alert the unsuspecting tourists while the news hadn't spread yet, their conclusion was that the police force wouldn't be able to act on the matter as it would call too much attention to the investigation,but what about that gumshoe?, Chief Richart asked in an intrigued tone, Felix Grey, the weird man who had moved to town the year prior,well... he is a flatfoot from the apple southwest of here, right?, Ambrewster questioned, while Charles only nodded. What a perfect idea, they could simply use the excuse that one of the victims family had hired him if anyone questioned their affairs while the investigation happened under the table.

While during the day the resort looked pristine and beautiful, almost like stepping inside a movie set, at night it almost seemed like walking right into a nightmare, the people that lived in that picture perfect town were no different, and Grey learned that very quickly. Munar, the pudgy man who ran the Snowy Rest inn, a big mitteldeutsches style building that had quite a few rooms that were always occupied by the tourists, as nice and good spirited as he seemed, Felixs first impression of this man wasn't of the best, his shady business practices of raising prices to unsuspecting customers was actually not only his, many of the business around town did this sort of trickery for a pretty penny, an even better example of that sort of thing where the two siblings that ran the small Igloo shaped shop, Mika and Carassa, inside the Ice Cavers - a very popular attraction, the two little pests would incessantly nag the costumers around until they got angry and went away or bought something to make the them stop. Somehow, even with this sort of matter being quite well known amongst the tourists, business was still always blooming in this eccentric little town.

Not much is known about the weird cult-ish behavior of the long time residents, it doesn't seem to interfere with the business at least, but they sure act weird. Every Sunday night, at around 11:30 PM, most - if not all, of the locals gather at top of the mountain and chant weird things together while lighting candles. No one from the outside seems to know about any of this, but they do believe that this town is the chosen it to receive the "divine intervention", as they call, meaning they, and only they, will be spared from the impeding doom that is right around the corner. To receive this "blessing" the people involved in the cult dedicated their lives to the "grand master", an unknown man that no one seems to know - most likely the resorts owner -, this meant "ascending" to the heavens and "abandoning their physical form" to be guided into the "next level". Most of the names used around town were not actually real, they are chosen by each member themselves to signify "leaving" their older selves behind and entering a "new form" that will be ascended.

January 1st, 1915
Yesterday father gave me this old Oliver #5 typewriter for my 15th birthday, it does not seem to be in bad condition for the amount of use it must have seen at the journal with my father, but I'm happy to be able to use it.
I know how much our family has been struggling to earn any money, maybe now I can try and start writing to sell some books. I do not know how to create a proper story, but as long as we can feed ourselves that is enough.
I might as well use this to machine to pour my bottled feelings out, so I mark this as the begging of a diary.

February 20th, 1915
For the past month I've been constantly writing a book, I haven't told neither father nor mother about it, they would call it insanity. Father has been working twice as hard as before, he has been able to make a bit more money from it, but none of us know how long this will last...
Is it too much to ask for a normal life like the other boys in my class? They have to much money to throw away, such disgraces of society...

February 26th, 1915
Went to the city today, my fathers boss at the journal agreed to publish my story in a small section, if other enjoy it I can keep writing. As compensation he gave me quite a bit of money, and mother was surprised to see it.
Lying to her feels horrible, but I can't tell them quite yet...

March 2th, 1915
Yesterday they published my story on the paper, people seemed to be interested in the murder mystery genera, the boys at school were all commenting about how interesting it must be to become a detective... I do wonder sometimes...
I was told to keep on writing this story, to keep on faking being this super secret detective that is trying to tell his story, but that is not what I care about, I'm only in it for the money.

the inspiration...

As any other creative my inspiration comes from many places, but Flusts story begins way before all this. On the 31st of December 2009 I created a pet called FlustGray, a green Gelert that I later painted blue, why did I create this pet, you may ask? Well, I had fallen in love with the art for the UC Grey Gelerts, but at the time UC trading was not only almost impossible but I also was only 11 years old, so I knew absolutely nothing about trading pets. The funny thing is that one thing hasn't changed in all these years, I had always loved creating characters for my pets, for Flust, it was a depressed and intellectual guy who had a somber past he never talked about, I wanted to start with what I had already layed out as an homage to that pet.

At the time I made a very bad looking petlookup with the art for the UC grey gelert and a few, now, weird keywords: fine, luxurious, united, silent, shy, winner, real, helper and younger, and even though I have no idea what those meant they did spark an idea inside my head. What time period what be more perfect for a character "hiding" their past than the luxurious 1930s and their noire movies? From my day to day appearance and aesthetics it may not seem like it, but I adore reading about history and the only thing I adore even more than that is fashion history! So much can be learned by how people used to dress and present themselves, and exprêssion through fashion and appearance has always been one of my favorite subjects as a whole.

I guess now we can begging with the other inspirations for this character! One of my biggest hobbies is gaming, so I can clearly see quite a few characters seeped into his own, the one that comes first to my head is for sure Kyoko Kirigiri from the Danganronpa: Trigger Happy Havoc, a girl who starts the story with amnesia and seems to know quite a bit about doing detective work and solving riddles. Another character that seems to have a bigger baring on the story itself other than on Felix is Curtis Craig from Phantasmagoria 2: A Puzzle of Flesh, one of my favorite retro games, the plot focus on Curtis and his seemingly normal life as inexplicable things start to happen.

However, fiction is not the only thing that has inspired me to create his character. As I've mention, history is one of my favorite subjects, and there's a good reason I chose Terror Mountain and a skiing resort, in the 1930s and 1940s skiing became a huge thing, and you can still find quite a few posters and ads from the time depicting the scenery, the funny contrast of girls in shorts and crop-tops while in the snow having fun really reminded me of Terror Mountain as a whole. The cult aspect of the story is also inspired by real life stories of the Heaven's Gate Cult, which believed that aliens were superior being that should be venerated and the only way to go to heaven was going to space. They did live in a small community retreat but outsiders would not be able to enter.

about me...

Offline

why...

a uc grey gelert...

Now, your next question may be, why in all these years didn't I just paint a random gelert grey and slapped this story on them? To be completely honest, I don't know, but something about the converted grey gelerts just... didn't click, I never painted the actual FlustGray because paint brushes where something very very expensive at the time, and the one and only FFQ I had ever gotten turned my starry Kougra, Tharuxi into a Faerie Kougra (a very popular and expensive color at the time), and after losing my account and moving to this one I never again thought of Flust...

That was the case until I created Bjarkas character, after getting her from the pound because of how much I loved her name, I made a character to complement Abraxiths character, a young witch attending a magic school, Bjarka would be one of the "mean" girls that actually grows to see Abraxiths side of the story, but it didn't feel quite right for Bjarka to not have a companion of some sort, that was when I created Ethan, who whould be her significant other, at first he was a gelert, and that's when it hit me again, "a grey gelert", why not? I thought at the time, but no matter how much I tried, it simply never worked. Something was always off, no matter how many times I tried to work out his character, so I just left the idea there to rot.

After starting to clean up my accounts from the pets I ended up not creating characters for and trying to trade them for others that would inspire me I noticed how I had already attained most of my pet goals, be it painting or morphing or zapping, so I had to start thinking of a new goal, and since UC trading was so popular I decided to look at the pets to maybe consider getting one, and so it all started again, I saw the grey gelert and everything came rushing into my head, Flust, how much I wanted him to be a UC grey gelert and his whole character. In that epiphany I began to work on this exact page, it was as if every detail of his story came to me naturally, the aesthetics, the words, the mood, everything, and just like that I knew what I needed to pursue now, I needed a UC Grey Gelert!

Also after doing a whole run of RE:Creation and completaly finishing Roginol's page I actually felt like I had a chance of making a good project / character page, which is how all of this began!

me...

Your last question regarding this whole project page is probably why me? Why should I be chosen to have such a rare pet that so many others are seeking in this very moment as well? I'll be very honest while answering this question. There is no real special reason I deserve him, and yet I've devoted hours of my life creating this project and this character only based on my passion for the things I've written. As I've mention in the last section, the idea of having a grey gelert has been on the back of my mind at least for the last 3 to 5 years, and yet I've never been able to work it all out until the moment I began working with this specific UC in mind.

My passion for writing and character creation was and still is what drives me to stay on neopets, this site still has a lot of meaning for me even after so many years have passed. I've meet and lost friends in this 11 years, but I still cherish the many memories that shaped me and my characters as they are today, which is why I hope to continue to develop not only Flust after getting him but all of my characters in general, I don't want my pages to stagnate in time and my characters to be the same forever.

With all of this, and knowing that there are so many other incredibly creative and artistic people in this site that deserve such beautiful pets, I still believe my drive to create and develop incredibly intricate stories and worlds is enough that this pet will be very much cared for and loved until the end of this website as a whole.
